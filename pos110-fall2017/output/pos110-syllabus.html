Catalog Description

An introduction to the major features of the policy making process in the United States. Emphasis on the structures and institutions of the American political system and the role of citizens in political process. Examination of democratic theory and political philosophy in the American context. Meets new General Education Social Science requirement.

Assignments

Political Autobiography

Due:: Mon Sep18
Assignment:

Write a 750-1000 word autobiography of your political background and understanding. Describe your political ideology in terms we've used in class, placing yourself on the political spectrum.Review the impact your family, schooling and other sources of political values on your political socialization. Explore your views of the current political climate, detailing your own involvement (or non-involvement) in the political process (global, national, state, local). Be careful to reference the class readings to date. Be sure your essay is well-written, with appropriate paragraphs, grammar and spelling. Submit your essay to the appropriate Blackboard assignment dropbox.

Grade: This assignment will contribute 15% to your course grade

My American Exceptionalism

Due:: Mon Oct16
Assignment:

Write a 1000-1250 word essay exploring the topic of "American Exceptionalism" and how it relates to your own political life. Define what is, for you, exceptional about the United States, using the terms we've discussed in class. Consider and discuss your view of America's place in the world, and its current political climate. To what extent do you believe the US is a democracy? What are the core values of our country? My Key Issues

Due:: Mon Oct30
Assignment:

Write a 1000-1250 word essay identifying the issues that are most important to you personally. You should identify five issues that you will focus on for the second half of the semester. In your essay, identify the factors that make these issues personally important to you. Summarize the current state of each of these issues in the US. Discuss the ways in which your views on these issues are informed by your own ideology and values, and place them in context of contemporary political debate.

Grade: This assignment will contribute 15% to your course grade

My Issues in the Contemporary American Political System

Due:: Mon Dec11
Assignment:

Write a 5000 word paper exploring each of the five key issues identified in your "My Key Issues" assignment. For each issue, write about 1000 words summarizing the issue and dentifying the relevant political actors focused on the issue. Be sure to reference interest groups active in the issue arena, and specify the current state of play in Congress, the Executive branch and the Courts.

Grade: This assignment will contribute 45% to your course grade
